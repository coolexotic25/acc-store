Refund Policy

We do not provide refunds in most cases and will just provide a replacement if there is any problem with your account although if it is a problem on our end that we cannot solve we will offer you a refund or another product with the same value as long as you return the account to the original email.

Warranty Policy

We offer a lifetime warranty policy although some of the things below may cause your warranty to be revoked:
• Changing Accounts Email
• Account banned after you bought
• If you contact support of the actual game company with account issues that may lead to getting the account locked before contacting us first
• Abusing replacements
• Chargeback/Dispute/Negative Feedback without contacting us beforehand

What warranty does not apply to:
• Bonus Games that may come on your account
• Any game progress you had on your account and we are not responsible for any lost progress
• If the account is in another region that you don't like

It is best to check the account for problems before changing any details to ensure there is no problems with it so you can get an easy replacement if there is a problem, to receive a replacement the account with issues/problems must be restored back to the original login including email and password and any other security measures to be disabled to avoid any abuse and misunderstanding.

Contact us and we will solve your issue without having to get paypal involved, if you try to scam us and get your money back your account will be locked and we will make sure to win the dispute leaving you with nothing so please don't :)

Negative Feedback

PC Requirements

Some games may have higher PC Requirements so please make sure your PC can ran the game before buying an account from us, as we do not provide refunds in these cases as this is a problem on your end, although this is still covered by a returns policy below.

Returns Policy

If you no longer want the account you ordered, as long as you return all the data back to the original, we will take it back in return for 50% off the price you paid. This does not apply to products such as random steam keys, random steam accounts, xbox game pass code. We still have the right to reject returns requests for no reason.
