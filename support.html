How to change your Minecraft Account Email?

Please note, if the account you bought has been stocked recently you will have to wait up to a week before microsoft let's you change the email.

This is a Microsoft Account so we will be changing the microsoft email.

  1. Go here (There may be some security measures such as password/email code before you can access the page)
  2. Select "Add a new way to sign in or verify"
  3. Select "Email a code"
  4. Enter the new email you want the account changed to and then click next
  5. You will recieve a code in the new email you entered, copy it and paste it and click next
  6. You should now have added your new email and be back on the same page as you were from step 1
  7. Under "Ways to prove who you are" you can now remove the old email and just keep the new one
  8. Go here
  9. Select "Add email address"
  10. Select "Add an existing email address as a Microsoft account alias" and enter your new email and select "Add Alias"
  11. Beside the newly added email select "Verify" and "Send Mail"
  12. You will then recieve a link in your new email, click it
  13. Select "Make primary" beside the new email
  14. Remove old email
  15. Your done!

How do I change my steam account email?

  1. Go here
  2. Under "Contact Info", select "Change my email address"
  3. Select "Email an account verification code to..."
  4. Enter the code received to the accounts current email and click "Continue"
  5. Enter your new email and click "Change my email address"
  6. You will now recieve a code to the new email, enter it and select "Confirm Email Change"
  7. Done!

How do I activate my Xbox Game Pass code?

Requirements:
• Card/VCC (We do not provide this)
• USA VPN (Here is a free one: https://brightvpn.com)
• USA Region (Change in Microsoft Account Settings)

  1. Connect to USA using your VPN
  2. Sign in with your Microsoft Account at https://www.microsoft.com
  3. Go to https://account.live.com/editprof.aspx
  4. Change "Country/Region" to "USA"
  5. Copy the key found at the bottom of this email
  6. Go to https://redeem.microsoft.com
  7. Paste the copied key
  8. Click "Continue" and in the next window "Confirm"
  9. You will need to add a Card/VCC (You won't be charged)
  10. Your 1 Month Subscription is now activated!
  11. Done! You can now change your region back and turn off VPN
